Pasta with pesto sauce

Description

You can make this recipe with homemade pesto or store-bought pesto. I used store-bought pesto for a quick and easy meal to prep. I love making pasta al dente for a delicious Italian feel.

Ingredients

  • Cooked pasta
  • Garlic
  • Pesto
  • Chili flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Heavy cream
  • Milk
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. In a large skillet add olive oil, minced garlic, cook it until lightly golden brown
  3. Add heavy cream, milk, basil pesto, chili flakes, salt and pepper, and grated parmesan cheese, and mix it
  4. Add pasta to the sauce
  5. Enjoy!
